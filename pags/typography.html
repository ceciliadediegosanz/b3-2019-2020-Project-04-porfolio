Typography

In this section you will find different projects related to typography.

In each one we see a different typeface treatment, always treated from a manual basis but with different results.

The warmth of these projects is a very important characteristic and it is what makes them so special.

Vivienne Westwood

The artist's book about punk designer Vivienne Westwood is for me one of the most special projects I have. It is the first important project that I carried out at the university and that was exhibited in Matadero Madrid.
It consists of a series of photographs taken by me in the streets of Madrid printed on the main support of the project, the cloth.
The handwritten typeface is hand-drawn.Both the accessories and the photographed trends and materials used refer to the punk style of the iconic Vivienne Westwood.

Todo era azul

Everything was blue is the representation and interpretation of the poem of the same name by the Spanish peta Miguel Hernandez.
The manuscript treatment on different tracing papers manages to capture the depth and subtlety of his work.
