La Goyesca

La Goyesca is a Km 0 brand that bases its sales on products from nearby producers, with a radius of less than 100km.

LUME fest

Lume Fest is a visual instalation festial that took place in the small town of Fuentesoto, Segovia.
The idea behind this project was to gring together the enviroment of the countryside, the silence of the deep night with the enegy produced by the electronic music and strong lights from the instalations.

Batu Food Truck

Batu is a Basque food truck.
The objective of this project was to show the culture of the Basque country, both Spanish and French.
From the first moment we tried to capture the curved shapes and colors of the wonderful Basque sea, the elegance and serenity of its culture and at the same time give it a youthful and somewhat closer air.

Bordeaux Vin

Bordeaux vin is a project to reinvent the identity of the Bordeaux wine brand.
The different types of wine are represented in an abstract and geometric way thanks to their colors.
Our goal was to bring wine closer to youth and show them that it can be as suitable for daytime as it is for the night. That is why each bottle has a label for the day version that if we take off it shows us the one ideal for the night.
