Editorial
design

In this section you can find some of our editorial design projects.

The two examples you will see below are two magazines. One, written in Spanish, based on food and fair trade production and the other very personal and written in French.

Paulette

Paulette is a French youth magazine that this time I have completely redesigned so that it only talks about me.
The objective of this work is to make a publication as personal as possible.
To achieve this, all the texts are written by me and speak of my curiosities. The photos are either taken by me or me, etc.

Madrid a la carta

Madrid a la carte is a very careful magazine that focuses on fair trade, km0 products, etc.
On its pages you can find both interviews with people of interest in this field, as well as curious information and very relevant information and advertising for stores and brands that keep the same principles.
